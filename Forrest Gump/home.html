Forrest Gump

The movie Forrest Gump is about a man named Forrest Gump. The movie was released in 1994 yet still holds up to the standards of a good movie today. Forrest is a man with relatively low IQ (Intelligence Quotient), but his pure heart and unwavering kindness make him endearing to everyone he meets. His journey through pivotal moments in American history showcases how determination and simplicity can lead to extraordinary achievements and lifelong connections with diverse people.
